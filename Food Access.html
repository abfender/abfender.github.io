Project Plan¶

Project Description and Goals: If we use this dataset, we will examine the disparities in food access in Louisiana. We will investigate if any factors play a role in food access, such as access to a vehicle, income level, age, racem and receival of SNAP benefits. We aim to compare these different factors against each other, as well as to compare multiple factors against each other in order to determine correlations.
